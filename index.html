Instructions

A transmission chain is the sequential passing of information between several people, each person in the chain conveying the information they were given to the next person.

Data passing through bodies.

People serve as a filter, modifying the information. The way that the information transforms through the transmission chain tells us about the participants and the information itself.

After you contribute your part in the chain, switch with another person. You can go back and forth with a friend or just leave the page unattended for the next passerby.
